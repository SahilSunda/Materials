Company : Jumio Corporation
Company Name : Jumio Corporation
Nature Of Business : Digital Identity Verification
Designation : DevOps Engineer
Tentative Job Location : Bengaluru
Description :

Company
Jumio is the future for online and mobile ID verification. We are the largest and fastest growing company in the ID verification space. With a global footprint, we’re expanding the team to meet strong client demand across a range of industries including Financial Services, Travel, Sharing Economy, Fintech, Gaming, and others.

Role Purpose:

You will be helping companies answer a fundamental question - are people who they really say they are? Digital identity is a core use case and 100s of top brands already trust Jumio to underpin their customer on-boarding, KYC and AML. There are multiple and growing use cases across Financial Services, Sharing Economy, Retail and Gaming. At Jumio, you will work for one of the market leaders in the global identity verification space that is helping to make the digital world a safer place for everyone.

As a DevOps Engineer, you will help design elegant systems infrastructure that is secure, robust, elastic, cost efficient and manageable. And you will do it using the best of breed public cloud infrastructure - we are deployed in all the three major public clouds, AWS, GCP, and Azure. We have a modern tech stack, and our DevOps engineers are more developers than operations people. You will fit right in with us if you have a passion for designing system infrastructure, love writing code, and are continuously thinking about building reliable systems.

Role Value:

By designing and developing systems infrastructure that is manageable, self-reporting, secure and cost-effective, you will have an impact on the efficiency, security and cost of running our systems. Your passion will enable us to save costs, secure the PII that we save on behalf of our customers and end-users, and differentiate us from competition in terms of levels of uptime and reliability.

Example Responsibilities:

  • Deployment, management, and administration of web services in a public cloud environment
  • Design and develop solutions for deploying highly secure, highly available, performant and scalable services in elastically provisioned environments
  • Design and develop continuous integration and continuous deployment solutions from development through production
  • Have direct impact on running a business by thinking about innovative solutions to operational problems
  • Drive solutions and communication for production impacting incidents
  • Partner well with development and business teams across continent

Experience and Qualifications :

  • Passion for programming in any programming language - e.g. experience with C++/Java/Python
  • Decent knowledge in Computer Organization, and basic operating system concepts, whether experimental or from books
  • Decent knowledge in Data Structures, Algorithms
  • Love to set up systems and play with them; and in doing so gather insights about how systems work
  • A logical way of thinking and passion to automate systems administration tasks
  • An attitude to learn and improve self; team; and systems
  • Someone who is not be afraid to challenge oneself and one’s team with ideas even if they are counter to what the group is thinking
  • Willingness to learn and apply new technologies and ideas in innovative ways.
  • Have excellent analytical, conceptual and communication skills in spoken and written English, appropriate for an international team environment

Great to have Experience and Qualifications:

  • Experience with SQL, GIT, Linux, AWS, GCP
  • Experience with bigdata systems - such as Hadoop and Spark

Key Characteristics and Attitudes:

In a recent global survey these attributes were valued by Jumios in all locations and functions - we firmly believe in hiring for attitude as well as skill.

  • Friendly and supportive
  • Adaptable and flexible
  • Articulate and persuasive
  • High IQ and EQ
  • Curious and coachable
  • Commercially Aware
  • Resilient and tenacious
  • Big picture and the detail

Jumio Values

  • IDEAL: Integrity, Diversity, Empowerment, Accountability, Leading Innovation

Progression
At Jumio you have the opportunity to

  • Work in a collaborative and trusting environment allowing you to turn your ideas into reality
  • Be part of a strong international team, where everyone’s opinion matters
  • Have flexible working hours, and learning & development opportunities
  • Work in a meritocracy, where you grow professionally by excelling at work

@Work:

Jumio Bangalore is one of four R & D centres at Jumio, alongside Vienna, Austria, Palo Alto, California and Montreal, Canada. Located on the Outer Ring Road in Kadebeesanahalli, Bangalore, we focus on development of multiple products and data platforms for Jumio, based on a cutting edge tech stack. We deliver components that power Jumio’s market leading products. Jumio Bangalore is a diverse team of engineers from all over India that enjoy knowledge sharing, being curious and collaborating across borders.

Equal Opportunities

Jumio is a collaboration of people with different ideas, strengths, interests and cultures. We welcome applications and colleagues from all backgrounds and statuses.
