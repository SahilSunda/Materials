Description :

We are looking for a Pre-Sales Consultant to join the growing team at our AI-ML start-up. The ideal candidate should be passionate about technology and would want to work closely with business to solve real-world problems using Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, using data as the fundamental.

An MBA degree with previous experience of Pre-Sales and exposure to handling data and solving unstructured problems is preferred.

Roles & Responsibilities:

∙ Prepare marketing & sales collaterals for AI-ML products and solutions
∙ Work on key industry projects – such as Banking, Pharma & Healthcare, Retail, etc. to develop familiarity with business processes, systems & data

∙ Work closely with leadership team to keep track of sales pipelines & have client-ready content
∙ Work with client delivery & product development teams to identify gaps & potential solution differentiators for collateral development
∙ Understand existing use cases & conduct secondary research to tailor offerings
∙ Assist leadership team in customer proposals & RFP development
∙ Participate in pilot projects & POC, acting as the bridge between business & technical teams to understand requirements & deliver value

Skills & Experience:

∙ Must have prior knowledge in technology
∙ Hands-on-experience of working in the data / automation / advanced analytics / AI is preferred
∙ Strong analytical & communication skills in order to process and present information in n structured manner
∙ Awareness of handling social media – such as LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, etc. – is preferred in order to mobilise Finarb’s offerings through social channels
∙ Must take an active interest in AI solutions & technologies and the impact they can have across business domains
∙ Must be a team player, effective in working & contributing in a collaborative environment
