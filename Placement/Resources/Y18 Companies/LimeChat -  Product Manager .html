Company : LimeChat
Company Name : LimeChat
Nature Of Business : AI-Based SaaS Company
Designation : Product Manager
Tentative Job Location : Bengaluru
Description :

Overview

We are looking for an Associate Product Manager who is passionate about building products that customers love. You will join a dynamic and fast-paced environment and work with smart cross-functional teams to build products to move towards our vision of conversational commerce.

For an overview of the company, you can refer to this link ( https://www.notion.so/3eda2a8031694df6b9d00bd29460a034 )

Responsibilities

  • Suggest product enhancements to improve user experience
  • Coordinate with the engineering department to deliver functional solutions
  • Conduct research to identify customer needs and market gaps
  • Prioritize the implementation of new features and set specific timelines
  • Liaise with the Marketing department to ensure proper advertisement and positioning of new products
  • Monitor and report on metrics and user feedback after launching
  • Create support and training documents for internal and external users
  • Perform quality assurance controls on products
  • Participate in system configuration
  • Analyze competition (periodically)

Requirements

Must-haves

  • Ability to effectively collaborate with cross-functional teams
  • Strong logical thinking, grasp of basic product management frameworks such as root cause analysis
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Strong prioritization and time management skills
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Effective at execution - Willingness to roll up one’s sleeves to get the job done

Good to have

  • Previous work experience as an Associate Product Manager or similar role
  • Familiarity with market research, consumer behavior research, and competitive analysis
  • Knowledge of project management tools, like Jira or Trello
  • Engineering background

Why Limechat

Limechat is one of the fastest-growing SaaS startups in India today, having one of the best teams and working on one of the most exciting problem statements.

  • We give our team members (especially PMs) complete autonomy and ownership to execute their projects.
  • Since we are a small, fast-growing startup, we offer one of most exciting work opportunities with PMs being able to choose among multiple projects and even start their own new projects easily.
  • As Limechat grows, the responsibilities of its early team members grow as well, leading to extremely fast-paced career advancement

We also offer a range of benefits such as

  1. Company Credit Card
  2. Re-location Allowance
  3. Work from home is allowed
  4. Health Insurance
  5. UpSkilling Subscriptions (Udemy, Medium, etc.) (25k / year )
  6. Wellness Allowance (3k / month)

Working hours and leave policy

No work timings - You are the master of your time (We do have team syncs once a day which you have to be a part of)

No leave policy - Take a leave whenever for whatever reason you want. You don't need to explain yourself to us.

However, with great freedom comes great responsibilities. If you have committed something to the team, we expect you to give it your best to make sure that commitment is done on time.

Steps to Apply

  2. Follow us on **LinkedIn ( https://www.linkedin.com/company/limechat )**
  3. Apply to this JD
