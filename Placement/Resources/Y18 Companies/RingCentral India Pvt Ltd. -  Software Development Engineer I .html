About RingCentral
RingCentral, Inc. (NYSE: RNG) is a leading provider of business cloud communications and
contact center solutions based on its powerful Message Video Phone™ (MVP™) global
platform. More flexible and cost effective than legacy on-premises PBX and video
conferencing systems that it replaces, RingCentral® empowers modern mobile and
distributed workforces to communicate, collaborate, and connect via any mode, any device,
and any location. RingCentral is headquartered in Belmont, California, and has offices around
the world.
