Developer Associate: Data Protection
and Privacy Engineering Team


PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES

Do you like working on technology problems which impact people on a global scale? Are you interested in creating brand new collaborative experiences by writing software for a rapidly evolving and expanding ecosystem?

We are an engineering center of excellence with a proven track-record of building enterprise-hardened, trustworthy services to our customers.
Trust is the ultimate currency in the cloud business. Questions such as “In which regions is my data stored?” and “Who has access to my data?” are frequently asked by companies when moving to the cloud. These questions have become even more critical with the emergence of a wide range of new local, regional and international data protection and privacy regulations. Many of these data protection regulations restrict the geographies in which enterprises may store, process, and access sensitive data. Globally, with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other stringent data sovereignty laws continuously evolving, there is an ever-increasing pressure on enterprises to establish robust compliance structures that can meet current requirements and be adapted to future changes as they come.
“Compliance & Data Privacy Engineering Services” team works on one of the most strategic topics in SAP which is the Data Privacy and Protection (DPP) on SAP’s Business Technology Platform (BTP).
The team helps manage different data privacy regulations like the EU General Data Protection Regulation (EU GDPR), California Privacy Act, China Cyber Security Law (CCSL)

Data Privacy Services act as the central framework for managing privacy of personal data on BTP. Objective of the team is to build cross platform reuse services in accordance with the SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP).

Many customers who are currently in the planning and execution phase of transitioning to Intelligent Enterprise, are optimizing their current system landscape. This activity can bring about a situation where many of the current production systems become future legacy systems. This leaves an important question of how to manage the systems in a cost effective yet compliant manner. To cater to these use cases, the team is expanding its suit of services to also cater to Data Management services.

EXPECTATIONS AND TASKS

•    Understand the concept of DPP reuse services in SAP Business Technology Platform
•    As a full stack software developer, you will be responsible for coding, testing, automation, deployment, quality assurance for service/applications which are being developed with best programming practices
•    You are expected to work on backend technologies like Springboot, NodeJS and frontend technologies like JavaScript and HTML5 along with the development operations tools like Jenkins
•    You will need to understand the containerization of microservices and their deployments into different runtimes like Cloud Foundry, Kubernetes
•    You will be required to work in a scrum and agile development team. Collaborate effectively with a team of enthusiastic developers to deliver the cloud solutions
•    You need to take up full responsibility for accepted tasks and therefore demonstrate a high level of quality, speed, reliability and efficiency.
•    Enhance skills beyond own core development focus and works on other tasks for the benefit of the team.

•    Excellent development and coding skills
•    Knowledge of Core Java, JEE, Java Script

Following additional skills would be an advantage:

•    Test Driven Development - TDD
