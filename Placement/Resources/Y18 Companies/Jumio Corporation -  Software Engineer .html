Company : Jumio Corporation
Company Name : Jumio Corporation
Nature Of Business : Digital Identity Verification
Designation : Software Engineer
Tentative Job Location : Bengaluru
Description :

Company
Jumio is the future for online and mobile ID verification. We are the largest and fastest growing company in the ID verification space. With a global footprint, we’re expanding the team to meet strong client demand across a range of industries including Financial Services, Travel, Sharing Economy, Fintech, Gaming, and others.

Role Purpose:

You will be helping companies answer a fundamental question - are people who they really say they are? Digital identity is a core use case and 100s of top brands already trust Jumio to underpin their customer on-boarding, KYC and AML. There are multiple and growing use cases across Financial Services, Sharing Economy, Retail and Gaming.

At Jumio, you will work for one of the market leaders in the global identity verification space that is helping to make the digital world a safer place for everyone. As a Software Engineer, you will develop cutting edge back-end software that will delight our customers and help expand the state of the art in this exciting domain!

Role Value:

By developing highly performant, robust and scalable web services and back-ends, you will have an impact on the success of the many products and initiatives being run from Jumio Bangalore. Your passion will enable us to super serve our customers with offerings that meet their needs in ways no one else in the industry does. We welcome you to challenge us with improvements to our software development processes, tools and technologies, and tech stack.

Example Responsibilities:

  • Be part of a cross functional team to work on continuous improvement of our systems with innovative technologies
  • Collaborate closely with the team to deliver features working from requirements/analysis through the development process into the production environment
  • Work with Product Managers to clarify and prioritize work, with QA Engineers to maintain and improve quality, and with DevOps engineers to support operational and troubleshooting work
  • Keep up to date with the latest technologies, evaluate new tools in deep tech space
  • Define and use with your team best practices to produce new features in the most effective way while continuously improving the quality of existing system

Experience and Qualifications :

  • Have solid experience with C++/Java/Python/Shell
  • Good track record with excellent knowledge in Data Structures, Algorithm, computer memory, basic operating system knowledge and programming skills
  • Work closely with business users and other developers globally.
  • Understand the business domain and requirements.
  • Work in a highly innovative technical team with plenty of opportunity to improve and grow your technical skills.
  • See your changes directly impact the business.
  • Willingness to learn and apply new technical and functional skills.
  • Be able to write good quality & secure code
  • Have excellent analytical, conceptual and communication skills in spoken and written English.

Great to have Experience and Qualifications:

  • Experience with any of the following: Machine Learning, Tensorflow, Pytorch
  • Experience with SQL, GIT, Linux, AWS, GCP

Key Characteristics and Attitudes:

In a recent global survey these attributes were valued by Jumios in all locations and functions - we firmly believe in hiring for attitude as well as skill.

  • Friendly and supportive
  • Adaptable and flexible
  • Articulate and persuasive
  • High IQ and EQ
  • Curious and coachable
  • Commercially Aware
  • Resilient and tenacious
  • Big picture and the detail

Jumio Values

  • IDEAL: Integrity, Diversity, Empowerment, Accountability, Leading Innovation

Progression
At Jumio you have the opportunity to

  • Work in a collaborative and trusting environment allowing you to turn your ideas into reality
  • Be part of a strong international team, where everyone’s opinion matters
  • Have flexible working hours, and learning & development opportunities
  • Work in a meritocracy, where you grow professionally by excelling at work

@Work:

Jumio Bangalore is one of four R & D centres at Jumio, alongside Vienna, Austria, Palo Alto, California and Montreal, Canada. Located on the Outer Ring Road in Kadebeesanahalli, Bangalore, we focus on development of multiple products and data platforms for Jumio, based on a cutting edge tech stack. We deliver components that power Jumio’s market leading products. Jumio Bangalore is a diverse team of engineers from all over India that enjoy knowledge sharing, being curious and collaborating across borders.

Equal Opportunities

Jumio is a collaboration of people with different ideas, strengths, interests and cultures. We welcome applications and colleagues from all backgrounds and statuses.
