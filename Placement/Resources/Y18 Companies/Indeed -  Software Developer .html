Software Developer - University Recruiting

Description

At Indeed, our mission is to build software that helps people get jobs. We’ve built a fast, efficient, and reliable platform that handles 5+ billion job search queries every month. We provide the simplest job search with the most complete results, the easiest way to manage your resume and apply to jobs, and a powerful mobile app that handles over 50% of our traffic. As we continue building more great products, we are looking for ideas from every field of computer science to keep pushing us forward. This includes machine learning, big data, distributed computing, natural language processing, information retrieval, user interface design, and other technologies. We are looking for developers who are passionate about coding, love intellectual challenges, are eager to learn, and want to work with a variety of technologies to solve interesting problems.

Joining Indeed as a software developer right out of school is not like a typical entry level job. You will have lots of ownership and the opportunity to make a big impact right away. We believe in autonomy, responsibility, and fast iteration. You will work together with a small product team to design, develop, test, and launch products.

We are addicted to data; we use it to make product and architectural decisions, as well as improve the results generated by our ranking and recommendation algorithms. You will get the opportunity to dig into massive datasets available at Indeed to analyze, inform, and develop solutions to the right problems.

Your objective will be to find simple, fast, resilient, and scalable solutions using back-end and front-end technologies that are best suited for the job. We embrace open source technologies and contribute some of our core software back to the open source community. As our fast-growing company changes and evolves, you will also have opportunities to move between projects so that we can work on what matters the most.

● Experience with large-scale, high-performance systems is a plus
