Company : Eugenie Technologies
Company Name : Eugenie Technologies
Nature Of Business :
Designation : Data Engineer
Tentative Job Location : Mumbai/B'lore/Gurugram
Description :

Engineer – Eugenie.ai

We aspire to shape industrial sustainability transformation through the power of AI. We work towards building a world where industrial and economic growth coexists with environmental sustainability. Eugenie helps organizations to become sustainable by making their operations reliable. Ensuring reliable operations requires handling large streams of data and drawing actionable conclusions from it – a task that can’t be done by humans, but is difficult to scale. Eugenie uses the power of machine learning or AI, big data to analyze and diagnose any issues and early warnings in the operations and determine the root cause for operation teams to act on them on time. We are transitioning into a high-paced and growth stage.

To enable that growth, we're looking for a Engineer to join our world-class team and help our customers gain the maximum benefits of the cloud.

As Engineer, you are a key member of our team based in India and the US.

Our customers are large enterprises and manufacturers including the world's largest oil and gas and mining companies, who are digitally transforming their operations to achieve higher agility, scalability, and innovation for their operations. In Eugenie, we use AI and IoT to make operations more reliable and efficient with our two SaaS products i.e., RayFinn and Papillon.

Your role will be a key driver in the following areas (and many more):

  • Building systems for data ingestion from IoT endpoints or other middleware systems such as SCADA and MES
  • Transformation and storage of vast amounts of data requires special expertise
  • Designing, architecting, and implementing modern cloud-based pipelines for our customers
  • Making data accessible and usable for Eugenie’s AI core and the consumption platforms
  • Building pipelines such that we can achieve high data/analytics throughput with high volume/velocity data

What do you need to be successful in this role:

  • A minimum of (0-2 Years) years of professional programming experience + hands-on experience in building modern data platforms/pipelines
  • Programming language: Python, Scala, and/or Go
  • Frameworks: Spark, Flink, Kafka
  • Databases: NoSQL (timescaleDB), Relational databases (columnar & row-oriented), GraphDB,
  • Data pipelining: ETL, ELT
  • Cloud platforms: AWS, Azure, GCP and their managed data services
  • Interest in or experience with machine learning, IoT, data streaming, time-series data is a big plus

Why should you join Eugenie:

  • Lots of opportunities to develop your expertise while working together with a set of brilliant and friendly colleagues coming from the domain and functional expertise
  • Be part of a team that is making AI create a planet-sized impact
  • We’re continuously developing our business – that means, we’re not stuck with the attitude of doing things as they’ve always been done
  • A chance to work on high-impact projects for high-profile customers from several industries using cutting-edge AI and engineering solutions
  • We work closely with the biggest players in the cloud industry: Amazon Web Services, Google, and Azure
  • We grow together! There’s a possibility to gather valued certificates and participate in world-class courses and training – choose from various courses to ensure continuous learning with a personal training budget! participate and represent Eugenie in international conferences and tech events
  • At the office, we offer a fully stocked kitchen with catered breakfast and lunch, table tennis and other recreation, quarterly team outings with food, drink, and music, and a fun team of great people. MacBook for work and unlimited privilege leave.
