About the position

We are developing a new way of analysing, visualizing and operating on data at massive scale. This includes the creation of a new platform that works across hybrid cloud environments. You will help to design and develop the product from the ground up. You will work with a small but very passionate and motivated team. If you’ve always wanted to work on something fundamental and not be limited with the baggage from existing software, this is the position for you.

Roles & Responsibilities in details

• Design and develop solutions that manage data across public and hybrid cloud

environments

  • Build solutions that manage data residing in public cloud created by cloud native apps

  • Define component architecture, evaluate feasibility, develop for quality and robustness

  • Optimize design for scale-out, performance and cost

  • Work directly with key customers as required
