Company : Micron Technology Operations India LLP
Company Name : Micron Technology Operations India LLP
Nature Of Business : Central Engineering
Designation : Central Product Integration Engineer
Tentative Job Location : Singapore
Description :

Responsibilities

Cost Reduction Responsibilities

  • Work with procurement, fabs, and Process and Equipment Engineering (PEE), Facility Shops, and others to reduce total costs, controllable (variable), non-controllable (semi variable) or fixed wafer costs.

  • Identify, implement, supervise and monitor cost related metrics.

  • Work with Cost Speed Execution Alignment (SEAL) Program manager and business partners on cost reduction road map strategies for the company and/or individual sites.

  • Ensure that cost reduction activities remain aligned with larger corporate strategic goals.

  • Lead or facilitate monthly global cost meetings. Provide data analysis as needed to identify prioritization of cost reduction opportunities.

  • Work with the PEE and Procurement teams to properly manage vendors/suppliers in order to maximize cost and quality benefits to Micron.

  • Find opportunities to improve and sustain business processes and practices that will allow Micron to be more cost effective.

  • Use partnership with Finance for cost reporting and consulting needs for financial analysis.

  • Prepare/lead/facilitate in Cost Reduction workshops.

  • Support the Cost SEAL by attending and participating in meetings and program portfolio activities.

  • Lead cost reduction projects (CC Projects)

  • Drive alignment between fabs to maximize and improve cost efficiency.

  • Improve Cost reporting tools, develop cost savings metrics.

Lead Projects and Programs

  • Demonstrate ability to move projects effectively through project lifecycle – propose, initiate, plan, execute, and Post Implementation Review (PIR) – while balancing time, cost, quality, scope, and risk/opportunity constraints.

  • Utilize strategic tools, such as K-T Project Management, and K-T Problem Solving/Decision Making, to plan and communicate project objectives and results.

  • Motivate partners, sponsor and all project team members to take ownership of project and its success.

  • Include business partners to promote synergy for successful activity and project execution.

  • Select the best candidates for project teams. Define partner teams.

  • Lead effort to standardize the application of global area systems, defining aligned core business practices.

  • Lead efforts to coordinate the business case requirements of systems as appropriate for your area and users of area data.

  • Support Cost SEAL manager by providing feedback and help with vision/strategy roadmap and timeline for area system development.

Communicate Effectively

  • Understand and use the best media (e-mail, SharePoint, Global Workforce Platform (GWP), PowerPoint, Tableau etc.) and techniques for transferring ideas, information, and updates to a particular audience.

  • Keep Cost SEAL manager, PEE, Procurement etc informed of cost agreements, projects, and efforts.

  • Communicate at least monthly directly with Cost SEAL program manager, sponsor, partners and team members on project status, progress and roadblocks or opportunities.

  • Offer and communicate strategic ideas and thinking to meet corporate objectives.

  • Build effective external and internal relationships between the Cost Reduction Group (CRG), each Fab site, IS, and other MCT members to broaden influence and increase learning opportunities.

  • Communicate relevant information to applicable WW groups - lessons learned, project repositories, summits, new technologies, conference proceedings, Best Known Methods (BKMs), and one-on-one site visits.

  • Promote worldwide sharing and transfer of area-specific domain knowledge.

  • Listen to customers’ and partners’ input or feedback and act to improve benefit to Micron and to meet corporate goals.

  • Demonstrate cultural sensitivity. Show respect for different cultures and languages. Learn to pronounce names accurately. Speak English slowly and clearly when interacting with non-native English speakers. Invite feedback to ensure understanding.

  • Use conference call etiquette and enforce during all meetings.

  • Communicate effectively with vendors/suppliers when required. Understand and use appropriate communication channels (other MCT members, Purchasing, etc.). Clearly and accurately communicate Micron’s needs and objectives to vendors/suppliers. Adhere to company ethics and confidentiality policies regarding vendors/suppliers.

Demonstrate Individual Competence/Technical Expertise

  • Recognize functional and business process gaps. Formulate proposals for closing them.

  • Provide technical consulting and hands-on assistance to Fab area teams to create and/or identify the best solutions, troubleshoot existing solutions, and deploy new solutions.

  • Define area software requirements and business case justification for IS. Act as the liaison between Cost Reduction Group (CRG) team and corresponding IS team; provide technical expertise to assist IS with understanding the CRG’s current and future needs.

  • Evaluate applicable area solutions, including third-party software and hardware selection, if appropriate.

  • Stay current with area-specific business practices at each site.

  • Develop and/or utilize current system to assemble, communicate, and propagate best known business practices from the global community using Global Cost Meetings, and other appropriate methods.

  • Provide periodic Benchmarking to identify gaps and opportunities for cost reduction.

  • Increase technical expertise by participating in classes and conferences, and researching existing literature and existing Micron expertise.

  • Manage or drive standardization between sites regarding cost related issues, reports, and systems through the System Workforce Allocation Team (SWAT) or other appropriate means.

Set Direction, Goals, and Performance Expectations

  • Promote a collaborative environment (ability to work in a team). Participate constructively in meeting-level discussions. Integrate well with other team members, even when opinions differ from your own. Create strong morale and spirit in team. Share wins and successes. Cultivate open dialog. Acknowledge team dynamics. Build on positive relationships and address negative ones.

  • Hold self and others accountable for meeting goals and action items from projects, meetings, and conversations. Complete tasks on time.

  • When leading meetings, drive them with purpose. When attending meetings, proactively participate. Use appropriate meeting etiquette.

  • Drive projects with limited supervisory input.

  • Set personal goals that are clear, measurable, and in-line with department and Corporate goals.

  • Constantly self-evaluate approach and goals.

  • Practice continuous self-improvement.

  • Demonstrate Micron behaviors and follow performance management expectations.

  • Display mediation skills. Exemplify leadership without authority.

  • Protect Micron’s intellectual property by completing and adhering to Micron corporate requirements.

Maintain a Safe Work Environment

  • Encourage a safe working environment.

  • Understand all safety concerns within area of focus, as applicable.

  • Maintain knowledge of equipment safety concerns with respect to applications and their possible effects.

  • Maintain knowledge of area safety policies and procedures as applicable.

  • Follow up with Safety Coordinator on any incident as applicable.

Requirements :

  • PhD/Masters/Bachelors in Electrical, Microelectronics, Physics, Chemical engineering. Relevant 2 years of working experience Wafer Fab process Integration.
