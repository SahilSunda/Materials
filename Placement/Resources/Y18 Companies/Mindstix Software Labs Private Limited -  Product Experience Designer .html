Company : Mindstix Software Labs Private Limited
Company Name : Mindstix Software Labs Private Limited
Nature Of Business : IT
Designation : Product Experience Designer
Tentative Job Location : Pune
Description :

Who We Are & What We Do:

We craft high-impact digital experiences for luxury retail, CPG, ISVs and hospitality sectors that bring unmatched value to our customers globally. Our work ranges from a simple problem statement from a customer that evolves into rich interfaces across platforms for consumers; to a project with complex systems for business-critical challenges. Areas of our expertise lie in design thinking, next-gen mobile experiences, AI bots, enterprise apps and nascent product ideas. We are a buttoned-down startup; an agile, high-velocity team with a 'can do' attitude - that's us.

Who We Work With:

Our customers include Fortune 500 enterprises as well as Silicon Valley startups. VMware, Staples, Restoration Hardware, Estée Lauder, Reckitt Benckiser, Emirates, to name a few. We are looking for Design Ninjas! Ninjas who are phenomenal at designing effortless, inspiring interfaces to amaze and engage our customers & their audience. We are looking to hire Product Experience Designers who are passionate about design and technology and want to build high-quality digital experiences and products. A strong portfolio that displays end-to-end design skills. A design degree/equivalent or proven track record of project ownership is strongly preferred. Experience with engineering and cross-platform mobile UX design would be an added advantage.

You Fit Right In If You:

  • Want to pioneer the future of experience
  • Are a broad-spectrum thinker – you know that different design processes call for different design contexts
  • Are empathetic towards users yet analytical for business
  • Design skills with sensitivity to user-system interaction
  • Bring process innovation to the table

You Will Get To:

  • Bring ideas to life - think it & we’ll build it. Really!
  • Design across multiple verticals - Enterprise, Retail, Hospitality, Banking, Sales, Pharma, etc.
  • Work directly with customers & users - understand their needs and deliver directly to them.
  • Be involved in all aspects of design - conceptualization to visual design and everything in between.
  • Have end-to-end ownership - concept to market, all the way.
  • Work on the latest in tech - Voice, Chat, IoT, AI, Mobile, and much more.
  • Work in a flexible, agile and fast-paced startup environment.

The Skills We Look For In You:

  • Thought Process — The ability to zoom in & zoom out based on the context
  • Craftsmanship — Bring interactive ideas to life that demonstrate a high level of visual composition, interactive usability, and creative problem-solving
  • Presentation, Communication & Storytelling — Present and explain interactive designs to both internal and client-facing teams in a thoughtful manner, sharing your insights & rationale compellingly
  • Process Innovation — Participate in multidisciplinary teamwork with Product Managers, Visual Designers and Technologists to bring phenomenal experiences to life
  • User Testing & Documenting — Partner with the product team to test concepts, assess product-market fit and share prototype experiences
  • Systems Thinking — Can think at all layers, from product (system) design, to interaction, to beautiful pixels

Tools You Will Use:

Whiteboards, Post-Its, Sketch, Figma, Miro, Invision, Zeplin, Principle, Photoshop, Illustrator. Insert-your- tool-here. We’re tool-agnostic and early adopters that jump to the best tools available.
