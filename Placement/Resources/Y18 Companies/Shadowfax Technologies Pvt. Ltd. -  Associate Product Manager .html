About Shadowfax :

Shadowfax is India's leading technology focussed on-demand delivery & logistics firm. Shadowfax has been growing multi-folds over the last few quarters with over 300  clients across 600+ cities and 7000 pin codes.

Shadowfax is on a mission to provide opportunities to the next million micro-entrepreneurs in the country through its platform. It has launched the “First-of-its-kind” and “Industry First” super app providing opportunities for these micro-entrepreneurs. Media coverage on the launch of the super app here .

Our last mile delivery fleet is fully crowdsourced. More than 30,000 delivery personnel come online daily for a few hours and earn about US$1.5 per hour.  Our delivery network also includes 4,000+ light commercial vehicles and 5+ lakh square feet of last mile space.

Our clients are present across sectors like e-commerce, food, grocery, FMCG, electronics, consumer durables, pharma, apparel, auto, BFSI and Insurance etc. Our delivery services are split across different verticals:

  • Hyperlocal – express hyperlocal deliveries
  • Next – assisted task management services cross utilizing our rider fleet
  • Connect – inter and intra city, forward and reverse delivery through hub and spoke network

All this is achieved through Best-in-class technology platforms leveraging emerging technologies like advances in AI/ML, Advanced Robotics, Block-chain.

Shadowfax was founded by IIT Delhi alumni Abhishek Bansal and Vaibhav Khandelwal in 2015. Our leadership team combines world-class talent and entrepreneurial experience gathered at some of the leading companies and academic institutes. The company is well-funded by marquee investors like Eight roads, Flipkart, IFC (financial arm of World Bank), NGP, Mirae Assets and Qualcomm Ventures.

Working at Shadowfax

Shadowfax believes in an open, intellectually honest culture where everyone has the autonomy to create and make an impact. At Shadowfax, you will have an opportunity to solve some of the complex and new problems, build your competence and be a part of an innovative design-and-tech startup that’s revolutionizing the logistics industry.

We foster a culture that values first principle thinking, encourages constructive feedback, engagement, inclusion and diversity at all levels. Our core values define our identity and form the basis of our actions and reflect in everything that we do at Shadowfax.

As Associate Product Manager at Shadowfax you will:

At Shadowfax, you will be at the core of a massive growth story and opportunity to build tech-enabled innovative products which will impact millions of livelihoods.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Define and own the Product Roadmap and Strategy aligning and shaping the Business goals.
  • Build a deep understanding of our partners, their aspirations and become their advocates.
  • Collaborate & work closely with internal and external stakeholders to identify innovative opportunities that strengthen and differentiate our offerings and make Shadowfax the most preferred platform for our partners.
  • Collaborate with the Operations, Engineering and Design team to build products that deliver business value for our Partners.
  • Prioritise projects, lead product initiatives and ship features. Measure impact and share learnings with the team.

Skills Required:

  • Ability to think strategically and execute with a keen eye for details
  • Demonstrate strong problem-solving skills.
  • Should have an ability to dive into data, do customer and market research and come up with insights
