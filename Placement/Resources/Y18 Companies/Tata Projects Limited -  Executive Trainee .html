Description :

JD - Executive Training Program 2022-2023:

Students recruited from the premium IIT’s will join Tata Projects as Executive Trainees (ET’s). The Executive Trainee program (ETP) is the flagship program of Tata Projects where all the ET’s will be groomed for techno-managerial role.

The ETP is designed to develop excellence in project management and business leadership skills with a blend of structured classroom training and on the job experience.


Post the successful completion of ETP and the evaluations, the ET’s will be given posting in different SBG’s of Tata Projects.


Role and Responsibilities:

1. Trainees will be required to learn all learning objective given to them during the training program.
2. They expected to meet the deadlines.
3. They are required to achieve all set activities required to complete the learning objectives.
4. They are expected to perform in evaluations.
5. They are required to achieve min. 70% percentile to remain in the Program.
6.They are expected to follow all company policies.
7.They expected to follow all directives given by their project guides and HR.
