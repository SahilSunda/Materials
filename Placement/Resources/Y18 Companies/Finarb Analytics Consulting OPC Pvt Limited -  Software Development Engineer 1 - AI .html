Company Name : Finarb Analytics Consulting OPC Pvt Limited
Nature Of Business : AI and ML based management consulting and software development
Designation : Software Development Engineer 1 - AI
Tentative Job Location : Kolkata
Description :

Responsibilities

Software Development Engineer 1 - AI (SDE 1 – AI) will work with teammates to solve problems and build innovative AI solutions. You will also be expected to demonstrate an ability to learn and adopt relevant new technologies, tools, methods and processes to leverage in your solutions.

  • Applies engineering principles to solve complex problems through sound and creative engineering.
  • Quickly learns new engineering methods and incorporates them into his or her work processes.
  • Seeks feedback and applies internal or industry best practices to improve his or her technical solutions.
  • Demonstrates skill in time management and completing software projects in a cooperative team environment.
  • Write concise, robust, and clean code.

Required Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree (or higher in engineering, computer science or related field), or 1+ years equivalent alternative education, skills, and/or practical experience.

Fundamentals:

  1. Excellent problem-solving abilities with strong analytical skills.
  2. Understanding of operating system fundamentals.
  3. Understanding of scalable computing systems, software architecture, data structures, and algorithms.
  4. Knowledge of network, distributed, asynchronous, and concurrent programming.
  5. Knowledge of professional software engineering best practices for the full software development life cycle.

Tools and Technologies:

  1. Programming experience with Python including object-oriented design.
  2. Experience with Database systems (Relational or NoSQL), Linux and Git.

Team dynamics:

  1. Passionate about delivering high-quality software.
  2. Self-motivated and a great collaborator.
  3. Demonstrate the ability to deliver tasks on time; also exhibit the ability to adapt to change.
  4. Provide estimates; author validation tests; provide timely issue resolutions.
  5. Communicate effectively to surface progress, risks and help needed within the project team.

Preferred Qualifications

  1. Demonstrated prowess in competitive programming.
  2. Experience with an existing machine-learning framework (e.g., Scikit-learn, TensorFlow).
  3. History of open-source contributions and working with open-source communities.
  4. API development using Django / Flask frameworks.
