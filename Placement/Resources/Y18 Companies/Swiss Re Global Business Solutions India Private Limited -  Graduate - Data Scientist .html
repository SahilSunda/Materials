Company : Swiss Re Global Business Solutions India Private Limited
Company Name : Swiss Re Global Business Solutions India Private Limited
Nature Of Business : Insurance and Re - Insurance
Designation : Graduate - Data Scientist
Tentative Job Location : Bangalore
Description :

About Swiss Re

Swiss Re is one of the world’s leading providers of reinsurance, insurance and other forms of insurance-based risk transfer, working to make the world more resilient. We anticipate and manage a wide variety of risks, from natural catastrophes and climate change to cybercrime.

At Swiss Re we combine experience with creative thinking and cutting-edge expertise to create new opportunities and solutions for our clients. This is possible thanks to the collaboration of more than 13,000 employees across the world.

We offer a flexible working environment where curious and adaptable people thrive. Are you interested in joining us?

About the team and the role

You will work with Senior team members and stakeholders in Underwriting, Reserving, Claims and Finance to help in building a 360° view on the business by providing solutions and services across the value chain across multiple business unit within Swiss Re. You must be willing to learn and use data science, statistical modelling and  actuarial methods and techniques to help develop insight as we make sure that our solutions and services help our clients grow and make commercial sense.

Data scientists and Actuaries  are a part of highly skilled teams who build pricing/retention/risk selection models and integrate them into analytical products for property, commercial & casualty lines of business across geographies.

Main Responsibilities

  • Work with senior team members to help build new predictive and prescriptive models according to business needs, along with :
    • Gathering and creating model framework
    • Exploring new data sources for model improvement
    • Create dashboards and implement models for business use
  • Build a clear and independent view on current pricing and loss adequacy
  • Maintenance of existing models (re-training, expansion, and parameter updates)
  • Willing to learn and use actuarial and data science techniques to develop insights and trends that can be used for risk selection

About you

Ideal Candidate Profile

  • You have highly developed numerical and analytical skill and are able to build assumptions, methods, and conclusions with focus to evaluate accuracy and uncertainty in models
  • You can translate your quantitative work into effective visualizations for high impact communication to develop insights and practical solutions
  • You have statistical programming skills (e.g. R, Phyton) and well versed with the latest modelling and visualization packages
  • You have data handling skills and knowledge of SQL would be an additional an benefit
  • Knowledge in multivariate non-linear modelling, but also in traditional modelling techniques (linear regression analysis, time-series analysis)
  • Knowledge in advanced actuarial models, geo-spatial models, econometric modelling along are a plus

Personal Skills

  • You have strong interpersonal skills and the ability to network in an interdisciplinary & multi-cultural environment
  • You can articulate and communicate your work effectively and be comfortable in presenting your work to senior team members and leaders
  • You are motivated to focus executing on delivering high quality results on time
  • You are keen to learn and apply advance actuarial methods and data science techniques
  • Self- starter who is methodical and well-organized
  • You work for the collective success of the team in close collaboration with senior team members. You are open and dependable; and demonstrate collaboration and intercultural competence

Educational level

  • Master's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Economics or equivalent quantitative/ statistical fields
