About the Organization :

Touch was founded on a simple idea: we deserve a platform that will inspire everyone to attain their financial independence . Touch aims to understand our users and their areas of competence to guide them along the financial independence journey through user competence-specific portfolio planning. Together, we are building a platform that will instill the right investing behavior in our lives.

We hope to achieve this by amplifying two very simple yet profound principles that you will hear great investors like Warren Buffet consistently evangelize:

  • Define your area of competence
  • Be extremely selective about investing in businesses(less than 7) that are high value, low cost, and within your areas of competence.

As technology continues to evolve in unforeseen ways, we believe it will play a central part in our mission.

Touch is seeking Machine Learning Engineers to help build and optimize the User Competency Model. The mission at Touch is to build relations with users and inspire them to attain Financial Independence. The ideal Candidate will work on the Algorithm to lead all the processes from Data Collection, Cleaning, and Preprocessing, to training models and deploying them to production. He/She will be passionate about Artificial Intelligence and stay up-to-date with the latest developments in the field and is a good problem solver.

Job Type : Full Time

Responsibilities

  • Undertaking business objectives and developing models that help to achieve them.
  • Develop highly scalable classifiers and tools leveraging machine learning, data regression, and rules based models
  • Suggest, collect and synthesize requirements and create effective feature roadmap
  • Apply expert software development skills to a wide range of ML-related coding projects
  • Adapt standard machine learning methods to best exploit modern parallel environments (e.g. distributed clusters, multicore SMP, and GPU)

Requirements

  • B.Tech/M.tech degree in Computer Science or related fields from a premiere Institute
  • Good to have a 2-3 years of experience
  • Should have excellent computer science fundamentals and a good understanding of architecture, design and performance.
  • Should have good knowledge of product security domain
  • Proficient in Java/Python, data structures and algorithms.
  • Should be hands-on in writing code that is reliable, maintainable, secure, performance optimized, multi-platform and world-ready
  • Ability to work independently and in a team with strong problem solving skills.
  • Good understanding of object oriented design and knowledge of product life cycles and associated issues.
  • Technical depth in operating systems, computer architecture and OS internals.
  • Technical depth in Cloud Computing, Cloud Platforms and Services architecture and design

Good To Have

  • Foundational knowledge in, and fundamentals of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence
  • Experience with ML Lifecycle, AI Ethics, ML Frameworks like TensorFlow, Caffe, Torch, and other similar frameworks
  • Experience with NLP, search ranking, collaborative filtering, recommender systems, reinforcement learning, and probabilistic ML
  • For the Backend: Experience with Django, rest APIs, Google Cloud, handling deployments
  • Experience with cluster managers and frameworks like Apache Mesos, Marathon, DC/OS
