Description :

As a Data Science at Micron, you will employ techniques and theories drawn from areas of mathematics, statistics, and information technology to uncover patterns in data from which predictive models, actionable insights, and solutions can be developed. You will interact with experienced Data Scientists, Data Engineers, Business Areas Engineers, and UX teams to identify questions and issues for data analysis projects, and improvement of existing tools. In this position, you will help develop software programs, algorithms and/or automated processes to cleanse, integrate and evaluate large datasets from multiple disparate sources.

This position is being offered in Technology Development Area.
